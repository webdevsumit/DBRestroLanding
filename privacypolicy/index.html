Privacy Policy

What personal information we collect from our website users?

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Name: To personalize the user experience and for account identification.
  • Phone Number: For account verification and communication purposes.
  • Email Address: For communication, notifications, and account recovery.
  • Restaurant Information: Details such as name, menus, and the types of food offered.
  • Razorpay Credentials: Specifically, the Razorpay ID and secret key, securely stored for seamless payment processing.
  • Customer Information (Future Plan): To offer enhanced insights into customer behavior and preferences.

How we collect the information?

We gather this information through the following means:

  • Registration: Users provide this information voluntarily during the registration process.
  • Settings Page: Additional details may be collected or modified through the user's settings.
  • Order Process: During the ordering process, customer information and order amounts are recorded for transactional purposes.

How you use any collected information?

The collected information serves the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Personal information is used to maintain and customize user accounts.
  • Order Processing: Order-related data is utilized for transaction processing and to enhance the user experience.
  • AI Model Development (Future Plan): Order-related data may be used, in an anonymized and aggregated form, to train AI models aimed at assisting in business growth.

Information Sharing with Razorpay

We share the Razorpay ID and secret key of the restaurant's account for seamless payment processing. Rest assured, these details are securely stored and encrypted to prevent unauthorized access.

How you keep information safe?

We prioritize the security of your information. Our platform employs robust security measures, including the use of SSL encryption, to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.
